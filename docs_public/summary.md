## What is this dataset?

This dataset contains data on the population evolution by age groups in 11 Latin American countries for the years 2000, 2010, 2020 and 2023. Countries included: Mexico, Brazil, Argentina, Colombia, Chile, Peru, Venezuela, Ecuador, Bolivia, Paraguay a

## Why does it matter?

This dataset contributes to open science by making research data freely available for reuse, analysis, and citation.

- Journalists and science communicators
- Students and educators
- General public
